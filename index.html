Here's a time-line of Bill Gates Life And Achievements:


  • 1955 - Born in Seattle, WA

  • 1967 - Enrolled at Lakeside School. Where he would experience working with his first computer

  • 1968 - Began programming with Paul Allen in the computer center.

  • 1973 - Entered Harvard University.

  • 1975 - Microsoft Was Found.

  • 1985 - The First Windows (1.0) Launched.

  • 1995 - Windows 95 Launched.

  • 2008 - Left his daily job at Microsoft.

  • 2010 - Pledged $10 Billion to deliver vaccine to the world's poorest countries.

  • 2015 - Spearheaded the formation of the Breakthrough Energy Coalition to fund clean energy initiatives.

  • 2020 - $1.75 billion was donated to combat COVID - 19.

  • 2021 - Bill published his book How to Avoid a Climate Disaster, proposing a plan to prevent the worst effects of climate change.
